Egypt

Egypt (officially: Arab Republic of Egypt) is a transcontinental Arab country spanning the northeastern corner of Africa and the southwestern corner of Asia across a land bridge formed by the Sinai Peninsula. Its population is estimated at more than 116 million, making it the thirteenth most populous country in the world and the most populous Arab and Middle Eastern country. It is bordered to the north by the Mediterranean Sea, to the south by Sudan, to the east by the Red Sea, to the northeast by the Gaza Strip and Israel, and to the west by Libya. The area of ​​the Arab Republic of Egypt is approximately 1,010,408 square kilometers. The inhabited area is 78,990 km2, representing 7.8% of the total area. Egypt is administratively divided into 27 governorates, each of which is divided into smaller administrative divisions, which are centers or sections.
